# Voliber Risk Rules

## Hard Rules

- No legal threats
- No credit reporting threats
- No harassment cadence
- No contingency pricing
- No collections language
- No discounts without approval
- No payment plans without approval
- No disputed items without client review
- No pretending to be a law firm
- No aggressive payment demands

## Escalation Triggers

- Customer disputes work
- Customer says they already paid
- Customer threatens chargeback
- Customer threatens legal action
- Customer asks for discount
- Customer asks for payment plan
- Customer is strategically sensitive
- Data is incomplete
- Tone risk is high

## Operating Posture

Voliber follows approved rules, templates, timing, and channels. Anything outside those boundaries is held for client review before the next touch.

The goal is disciplined movement and clean documentation, not pressure.
