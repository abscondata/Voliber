# Voliber Business Model

## Core Thesis

Voliber applies institutional queue discipline to small-business revenue leakage. Many firms already have open money in motion, but unpaid invoices, stale proposals, failed payments, and dormant leads age without a consistent operating owner.

## Target Vertical

Initial focus: B2B service firms and agencies.

- Marketing agencies
- Web design agencies
- IT service firms
- Consultants
- Small professional services firms

These businesses usually have cleaner invoice and proposal workflows, email-native follow-up, and fewer legal edge cases than field-heavy or consumer collections environments.

## Pipeline Review Offer

The Pipeline Review is the diagnostic entry point. Voliber reviews available exports or system views, classifies open revenue objects, identifies A/B/C priority queues, flags approval-required items, and recommends a short operating plan.

Deliverable: a concise review showing open revenue categories, aging patterns, follow-up opportunities, exclusions, and a proposed 45-day operating plan.

## 45-Day Revenue Follow-Up Pilot

The pilot tests the operating loop on a controlled queue.

- Confirm source data and approval rules
- Classify and prioritize open items
- Run approved weekly follow-up
- Log each touch and outcome
- Escalate exceptions
- Report movement every week

The pilot should prove whether the queue can be worked cleanly and whether the business wants an ongoing revenue desk.

## Monthly Revenue Desk

The Monthly Revenue Desk is the recurring service. Voliber owns the weekly cadence for approved revenue follow-up and reporting.

- Weekly queue review
- A/B/C prioritization
- Approved follow-up touches
- Exception handling
- Weekly movement report
- Recommendations for the next cycle

## What Voliber Is Not

Voliber is not collections. It does not use legal threats, credit reporting threats, aggressive payment demands, or contingency pricing.

Voliber is not an AI automation shop. It does not lead with software claims, autonomous systems, or fake proprietary models.

Voliber is not a VA agency. It is not general task support or staff augmentation.

Voliber is not admin help. It is a focused operating service for open revenue queues.

## First 30-Day Priorities

1. Keep the wedge narrow: B2B service firms and agencies.
2. Validate the Pipeline Review format with real exports and inbox examples.
3. Define approval rules and risk controls before any follow-up touches.
4. Build a repeatable 45-day pilot scope.
5. Collect concrete process learnings before making public claims.
