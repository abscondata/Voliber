# Voliber Operating Model

## Backend Operating Loop

Discover -> Normalize -> Classify -> Prioritize -> Act -> Observe -> Update -> Report

## Loop Detail

### Discover

Identify open revenue objects from invoices, A/R aging, proposals, failed payments, dormant leads, shared inboxes, and call records.

### Normalize

Convert inconsistent source records into a shared working format with enough context to decide the next action.

### Classify

Assign each object a type and approval posture so the queue is not worked as a generic task list.

### Prioritize

Group items into A/B/C priority buckets based on value, age, clarity, likelihood of movement, and relationship risk.

### Act

Run approved follow-up through agreed templates, timing, tone, and channels.

### Observe

Record replies, silence, payments, closures, disputes, and new information.

### Update

Adjust status, priority, next action, risk flags, and approval requirements.

### Report

Send a weekly movement report showing what was worked, what moved, what needs approval, and what remains open.

- Last touch

- Risk flag

## Metrics

### Movement Rate

The share of worked items that changed state during the reporting period.

### Decision Rate

The share of approval-required items that received a client decision during the reporting period.

### Time-to-First-Touch

The time between an item entering the working queue and the first approved follow-up touch.
