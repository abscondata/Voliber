# Qualification Scorecard

Use this framework to grade prospects before outreach or before offering a Pipeline Review call.

## A-Fit

Strong fit for the first Pipeline Review motion.

- B2B service firm or agency
- Sends invoices, estimates, proposals, or retainers
- Likely has open receivables, stale proposals, failed payments, or dormant leads
- Owner, operator, finance lead, or client services lead is reachable
- Enough deal size for recovered or reactivated revenue to matter
- No obvious legal, collections, or dispute complexity
- Can likely export basic source data
- Has a visible operational reason follow-up may be inconsistent

## B-Fit

Worth testing, but weaker or less clear.

- Plausible B2B service model, but revenue workflow is less obvious
- Smaller ticket size or lower likely recovery value
- Buyer is unclear or buried in the organization
- Data quality may be inconsistent
- Follow-up gap is possible but not strongly signaled
- May need education on what a Pipeline Review includes
- Could become A-fit after discovery

## C-Fit

Do not prioritize for the first sales push.

- Consumer debt
- Medical collections
- Aggressive collection need
- Low-value invoices only
- Messy dispute-heavy business
- Too small to support a paid review
- Too enterprise for an owner-led sales motion
- Needs legal, bookkeeping, or accounting work before follow-up
- Wants guaranteed recovery or pressure tactics

## Scoring Notes

- Prioritize A-fit prospects for manual research and direct outreach.
- Use B-fit prospects to fill the list only when the A-fit pool is thin.
- Do not spend time forcing C-fit prospects into the pipeline.
- When uncertain, grade down until a real signal appears.
