# Outbound Sequence

Target: B2B service firms and agencies.

Main angle: Voliber runs a weekly queue for unpaid invoices, old proposals, and dormant leads. It prioritizes the queue, follows up on approved items, logs every touch, and reports what moved.

## Subject Line Options

- Revenue follow-up queue
- Old invoices and proposals
- Quick question on follow-up
- Unowned revenue items
- Pipeline Review
