# Revenue Pipeline Review Service Scope

This scope defines the operating boundary for Voliber revenue follow-up work.

## Voliber Does

- Classify open revenue objects
- Prioritize follow-up queue
- Send or prepare approved follow-up
- Log touches
- Route exceptions
- Report weekly movement

## Voliber Does Not

- Act as a collections agency
- Threaten legal action
- Negotiate discounts without approval
- Create payment plans without approval
- Handle disputed items without review
- Provide legal advice
- Guarantee recovery
- Replace bookkeeping, accounting, or legal functions

## Working Boundary

Voliber works from client-approved source data, tone rules, approval rules, and contact channels. Items outside those rules are paused and routed back to the client for review before any further action.
