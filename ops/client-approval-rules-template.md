## Items Requiring Approval

- Discounts, credits, refunds, or write-offs
- Payment plans or changed terms
- Disputed invoices, scope, delivery, or service quality
- Strategic, sensitive, or owner-managed accounts
- Legal, tax, accounting, or compliance questions
- Customer hardship, cancellation, or complaint signals
- Any object missing required source data

## Hard Stop Conditions

- Customer asks Voliber or the client to stop contact
- Customer disputes the amount, service, or agreement
- Customer threatens legal action or public complaint
- Customer requests a discount, refund, credit, or payment plan
- Records conflict or the object cannot be verified
- Account is marked hold, blocked, excluded, or do not touch
