## Escalation Triggers

- Customer disputes the amount, scope, or service
- Customer threatens legal action or reputational harm
- Customer requests a discount, credit, refund, or payment plan
- Customer reports financial hardship
- Customer asks to cancel or stop service
- Account is strategic, sensitive, or owner-managed
- Internal records conflict or are incomplete
- Item may involve legal, tax, accounting, or compliance judgment

## Definition of "Do Not Touch"

- Customers or accounts Voliber should not contact
- Items in active dispute
- Items assigned to legal, accounting, or bookkeeping review
- Items owned by a named executive or account manager
- Items with incomplete or conflicting records
- Items involving refunds, credits, discounts, or payment plans
- Items with a relationship, compliance, or reputational risk flag
- Any object the client marks as hold, blocked, excluded, or do not touch
