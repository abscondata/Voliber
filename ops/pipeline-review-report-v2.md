## 8. 45-Day Operating Plan

### Week 1

- Normalize records.
- Confirm rules.
- Work A-priority items.

### Weeks 2-3

- Continue follow-up.
- Work B-priority items.
- Route exceptions.

### Weeks 4-5

- Review aging movement.
- Close low-quality items.
- Prepare renewal recommendation.

### Week 6

- Final review.
- Monthly desk recommendation if justified.

## 9. Risks and Exclusions

- Disputed items require client review before action.
- Legal threats are excluded and must be escalated.
- Chargebacks are excluded unless the client provides explicit handling rules.
- Unusual payment terms require review before contact.
- Incomplete records may limit actionability.
- Relationship-sensitive accounts require client approval before contact.
- Voliber does not guarantee recovery.
